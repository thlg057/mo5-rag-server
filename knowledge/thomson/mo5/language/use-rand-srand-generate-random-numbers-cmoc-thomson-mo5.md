# Use rand() and srand() to generate random numbers (CMOC, Thomson MO5)

Generate pseudo-random integers using CMOC's `rand()` and initialize the generator with `srand()`.

## Steps

1. Include `<cmoc.h>`.
2. Call `srand(seed)` once.
3. Call `rand()` when you need a random value.

## Notes

- The documented maximum is 32767.
